About Me

I received the B.S. and M.Sc. degrees in Computer Science from Shandong University, and the Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences, respectively. In October 2021, I received the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at the University of Texas at San Antonio under the supervision of Prof. Shouhuai Xu.

Research

My research interests include

  • Cybersecurity Metrics and Quantification

  • Cyber Attack-defense Emulation

  • Blockchain Security
