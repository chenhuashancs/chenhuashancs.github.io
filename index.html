About Me

I received the B.S. and M.Sc. degrees in Computer Science from Shandong University, and the Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences, respectively. In December 2021, I received the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at the University of Texas at San Antonio. My PhD supervisors are Prof. Shouhuai Xu and Prof. Ravi Sandhu. I joined the Institute of Information Engineering as an Associate Professor in 2022.

Research

I am always looking for self-motivated students/interns to join my research group. My research interests include:

  • VR/AR/XR Security & Privacy

  • Audio-visual-based Biometric Authentication
